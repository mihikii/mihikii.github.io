crazy cat facts:

cats roughly spend 18 hours per day sleeping, which means they spend 70% of their lifespan having a snooze

most cats have 18 toes, 5 on their front paws and 4 on their back paws. however, some cats can be born with extra toes, this condition is called polydactylism.

in 1894, thomas edision recorded the first ever cat video

in 1995, a green furred cat was born in denmark. its speculated that the kitten had consumed copper contaminated water to cause this effect, however we are still not too sure

cats have 230 bones, which is 24 more than an adult human at 206

cats have an extra organ that allow them to taste scent

male cats are likely to be left paw dominant, while female cats are likely to be right paw dominant

stubbs, an orange tabby cat, was the mayor of the historic district of talkeetna, alaska for 20 years

usually, kittens under 3 months old have no response to catnip

the pallas's cat are the most expressive cat species in the world, they are also my favourite

a cat uses their whiskers for measuring gaps to see if they can fit through them

they also use their whiskers to detect changes in the environment, specifically helping them navigate in the dark

cats have ultrasonic hearing, being able to hear up to 64,000hz, whilst humans can only hear up to 20,000hz

contrary to popular belief, most cats are lactose intolerant, and drinking milk can upset their stomachs

cats can see shades of blue and green, but have trouble seeing red

cats were domesticated around ~9000 years ago, where they helped humans with rodent control

similar to a humans fingerprint, every cats nose print is unique and can be used for identification

cats can jump up to 6 times their body length in a single leap
